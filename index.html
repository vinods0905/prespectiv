Industrial AI solution

Prespectiv Labs builds end‑to‑end industrial AI vision systems – calibrated optics and lighting, deterministic motion, edge/GPU compute and trained deep‑learning models – engineered for 24×7 production environments.

What it delivers

  • Higher defect‑detection accuracy using CNN / transformer models.
  • Cycle‑time‑optimised inspection pipelines for high‑speed lines.
  • Traceable images, dimensional measurements and SPC reports.
  • Standardised architectures deployable across lines and plants.

Applications

Text / Label Read

Read text or embossed part numbers and detect deviations from ideal values using vision.

Poka‑yoke

Use vision systems to build detection or prevention based poka‑yoke by identifying the right variations.

Identify & Count

Identify, count and sort parts at various speeds with automated visual inspection.

Part Inspection

Detect macro and micro surface defects such as scratches, dents, missing features and colour issues.

Paint Inspection

Check paint aesthetics and detect defects at speed on automotive and other painted components.

Measurement

Measure dimensions, gaps, positions and defect size using vision systems with accuracy down to 100 microns.

Tracking

Track events, products and packages within the shop‑floor using visual tracking.

Vision Guidance

Guide robots for pick and place, weld or cut operations with precise visual feedback.

Manufacturing & pharma vision systems

Pharma: Bottling inspection

Problem

Improve quality throughput by tightening capping, dosage and label verification via machine vision and automation.

Solution

Single camera system on a linear arm with additional sensors for label reading and cap‑tightness validation.

Key metrics

  • Cap orientation within 0.5 mm with 95% accuracy
  • Dosage accuracy of 0.05 ml
  • Label verification with database at 99% accuracy
  • Operation speed: 60 bottles / min

Medical: Filter defects inspection

Problem

During disposable filter manufacturing, dust particles enter filter layers and must be separated in a continuous flow.

Solution

Automated filter inspection system with AI on a continuous manufacturing line, covering 40+ defect classes and dimensional checks, with PLC, camera, custom lighting and sensor integration.

Key metrics

  • 10K parts per day handled by a single machine
  • Error rate: 8 parts per 10,000

Pharma: Remote data recording & analysis

Problem

Air quality inspection manufacturer in the USA needed to record and analyse data captured in remote locations.

Solution

Implemented data sync from hardware to mobile via Bluetooth, then to Azure database. Data is analysed and reported to US government agencies through dashboards.

Technologies

  • IoT, Azure, SQL
  • Django, Angular

Pharma: Manufacturing data analysis & recording

Problem

Pharma company in the USA needed to track production rate and errors, and monitor OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness).

Solution

Perspectiv Labs integrated vision‑based defect detection with the production database to monitor operator speeds, equipment performance and production rates, and to provide OEE dashboards.

Technologies

  • Python, PostgreSQL
  • Dash / Plotly dashboards

AI training automation

Many vision inspection systems require continuous training with real defect data, especially when solutions are deployed across remote factories.

Our web‑based AI training tool is derived from CVAT, hosted and customized by Perspectiv for end‑to‑end workflows. From capturing data on deployed systems, it auto‑annotates known defects, lets testers correct edge cases, and runs automated training in the background. Trained models are deployed to edge devices via cloud.

This significantly reduces manual AI training time and keeps customer models updated on real‑time data.

Typical hardware stack

  • Cameras and lenses from Allied Vision, Basler, Edmund Optics
  • Edge and GPU compute from NVIDIA, Advantech and similar vendors
  • Industrial actuators and pneumatics from partners such as SMC

Visual RPA

AI‑powered visual RPA reads on‑screen values, documents and forms using OCR + computer vision to automate repetitive desktop flows such as data entry, validation and reconciliation.

  • Pixel‑level screen capture with AI + OCR text extraction.
  • Automates form filling and system‑to‑system data transfer.
  • Ideal for finance, logistics and shared‑services operations.

Product photography automation

Automated rigs and workflows to capture consistent, studio‑quality product photos and 360° views with fixed camera paths and calibrated lighting for e‑commerce and catalogues.

  • Scene presets for jewellery, food, FMCG and industrial parts.
  • Repeatable compositions and angles across thousands of SKUs.
  • Inline QA checks to flag focus, exposure and visual defects.

Image / video to 3D

Robust pipelines that convert 2D image sets or video sequences into 3D meshes and textured models for digital twins, AR/VR and measurement‑grade visual inspection.

  • Supports calibrated photos, turntable videos and drone footage.
  • Outputs watertight meshes ready for CAD, game engines or web 3D.
  • Used for industrial parts, cultural artefacts and retail assets.
